Cover letter

Anaya Sharma · Visitor visa application · prepared with Passage

To the Visa Officer,

Please find enclosed the supporting documents for my temporary resident visa application to visit my sister in Toronto from 12 August to 2 September 2026. I am a senior analyst employed in Pune, India, where I live with my spouse and two school-age children, and I intend to return at the end of my visit.

The following documents are enclosed, in order:

  1. This cover letter
  2. Statement of purpose
  3. Letter of invitation from my host, with her status and address
  4. Employer letter confirming my position and approved leave, with recent pay slips
  5. Bank statements for the past six months
  6. Proof of property ownership in Pune

Anaya Sharma

Page 2 · Statement of purpose

Statement of purpose

Why I am visiting, and why I will return

I am applying to visit my sister, a Canadian citizen, for three weeks in August 2026. The purpose of my trip is a family visit. I have planned the dates around my approved leave and my children's school term.

I have been employed as a senior analyst at Infosys in Pune for six years, and my employer has approved my leave for this period and confirmed my position in the enclosed letter. My spouse and our two children remain in India, where the children attend school, and I am responsible for their care.

I own a flat in Pune, evidence of which is enclosed, and I maintain bank accounts in India with six months of statements provided. I have sufficient funds for the visit and will not seek work in Canada.

At the end of my visit I will return to Pune to resume my role at work and my responsibilities to my family. I respectfully request that my application be approved.
